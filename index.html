About KinDog

KinDog is your family's digital storytelling circle — a place where you can share meaningful photos and the stories behind them. We believe in the power of memory, healing through storytelling, and honoring life’s journey. Whether you're a veteran, caregiver, or just want to leave something beautiful behind, your story belongs here.

Our Story

As a disabled veteran living with chronic health challenges, I created KinDog to be a voice for people like me — those with stories full of love, loss, and perseverance. This is our place to be seen, heard, and remembered.
